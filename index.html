Hair-goo

The object of the game is to make money by squirting your tube of colour while avoiding tears of a stampeding clients.

SPACE INVADER TIPS: The clients cry randomly with tears flowing down the screen. If the tears hits you or if the clients reach the end, you lose. If all the clients are attended to, you win and you can restart the game to get a higher score.

GAME KEYS: Use the F key to squirt and the arrow keys to move left to right.
